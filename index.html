FAQ

What is this?
A tool made by c00kie that looks at a player's best runs (by rank) and returns a list of what maps have the most similar leaderboards (in terms of players). One way to think of the list is "these are the 50 maps with the most similar leaderboards to my best maps"
What does the score mean?
The exact number doesn't really matter, just know the higher the number the more similar it is to your best maps.
Didn't something like this exist before?
Yes, there was a tool by Larry? that existed before, however this project and site is my own work.
The results seem off. Why?
Might be outdated, may be an outlier, or you might play more wallpogo than you'd like to admit.
Is this for soldier, demoman, or both?
Soldier only. I don't care about demoman.
